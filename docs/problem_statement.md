## Business Context
Food delivery platforms like Zomato operate in a highly competitive market where delivery speed and reliability directly impact customer retention. A single bad delivery experience increases churn probability significantly.
## The Core Business Problem
A food delivery company is experiencing rising SLA breach rates (orders delivered beyond the promised 30-minute window) in metro cities. The ops team needs to understand what is driving these breaches and which zones, time slots, and restaurant partners contribute most to the problem.
## Stakeholders
    • Operations Team: Need to know which zones and time slots to fix
    • Restaurant Partners: Need to understand their performance vs benchmark
    • Delivery Partners: Need to know where demand surges are predictable
    • Growth Team: Need to understand how SLA breaches impact retention
## 5 Business Questions We Will Answer
    1. What percentage of orders breach the 30-minute SLA, and how does this vary by city?
    2. Which time slots (hour of day, day of week) have the highest breach rates?
    3. Which restaurant partners have consistently high breach rates despite high order volumes?
    4. How does SLA breach correlate with customer repeat order rate?
    5. Are there specific city zones where delivery time is structurally higher regardless of time or restaurant?
## KPIs We Will Track
    • Average delivery time (minutes)
    • SLA breach rate (% of orders > 30 min)
    • Order cancellation rate
    • Customer repeat order rate (30-day, 60-day)
    • Restaurant on-time fulfillment rate
    • Delivery partner average on-time %
## Definition of Success
This project is successful if we can identify at least 3 specific, actionable patterns that explain SLA breaches and make concrete recommendations to reduce the breach rate by a measurable amount.
